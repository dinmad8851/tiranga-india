**White**

* The central white stripe in the flag signifies purity, truth, and peace. 

* It embodies the ideal of honesty and transparency in governance. 

* It reflects India's aspiration for a just and harmonious society. 

* The color white also symbolizes the diverse communities coming together as one, emphasizing unity amidst India's cultural richness. 

* The white stripe, being flanked by saffron and green, represents the bridge between the courage of the past and the growth of the future.
