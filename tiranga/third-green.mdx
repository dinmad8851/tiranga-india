**Green**

* The bottommost green stripe represents growth, fertility, and auspiciousness. 

* It symbolizes the nation's commitment to sustainable development, environmental conservation, and prosperity. 

* Green is a reminder of India's agricultural heritage and its connection to the land.

* It also underscores the importance of nurturing the natural resources that sustain the nation. 

* The green stripe also conveys the hope for a vibrant and progressive India, where citizens work collectively to achieve economic and social growth.
