**Saffron**

* Saffron, the topmost stripe in the Indian National Flag, represents courage, sacrifice, and the spirit of renunciation. 

* It symbolizes the unwavering determination of the Indian people to stand up against oppression and injustice.

* It can be referenced similar to the saffron robes worn by monks exemplify selflessness and dedication. 

* This vibrant color embodies the fearless commitment of the nation's heroes who fought for independence and 

* It also signifies the willingness of its citizens to make sacrifices for a brighter future.
